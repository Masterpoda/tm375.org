Toastmasters 375
Fort Collins Toastmasters

About Us
Fort Collins Toastmasters is the oldest club in Colorado, founded in 1946. We are an open club and welcome all who want to improve their communication and leadership skills. We are a casual club but still follow the Toastmaster's meeting format.
Meeting location
Knights of Columbus Hall, 121 N. Meldrum, Fort Collins, CO: 3 blocks West of College and half a block North of Mountain. The K of C Hall is a white building on the west side of Meldrum with parking on the South side of the building.
Meeting time
Arrive at 6:45 PM for setup; meeting starts promptly at 7 PM. Meeting ends at 8:30 PM.
Membership fees
Like all TM clubs, we have dues:
  • Short version: $33 per six months.
  • Long version:
    • New members: one-time fee of $26. That's $20 for Toastmasters International and $6 for the club.
    • Each six months, every member pays $33. When pro-rated by month, that breaks down to $4.50 for Toastmasters International and $1 for the club itself.
