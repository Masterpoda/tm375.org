About Us
Fort Collins Toastmasters is the oldest club in Colorado, founded in 1946. We are an open club and welcome all who want to improve their communication and leadership skills. We are a casual club but still follow the Toastmasters meeting format.
Meeting location
Homestate Bank, 303 East Mountain Avenue, Fort Collins CO. We meet in the Community Room in the basement. NOTE: WE ARE MOVING TO A NEW LOCATION STARTING OCTOBER 4, 2016. Poudre Valley Hospital Lower level is likely location.
Meeting time
Tuesdays at 6:30 PM. Arrive at 6:00 PM for setup. The meeting starts promptly at 6:30 PM and ends at 7:15 PM. (WE ARE OPERATING ON A SHORTENED AGENDA DURING THE SUMMER)
If you arrive and find the doors locked, you can enter through The Parlour hair salon just east of main bank doors. From there, go to the elevator and proceed to the community room in the basement.

Membership fees details
Like nearly all TM clubs, we have dues:
  • $36 per six months, and is pro-rated as needed.
  • $0 of that goes to the club.
  • The remainder goes to Toastmasters International to cover the monthly magazine, help fund conferences and similar administrivia and to promote interests in common.
  • Toastmasters International also imposes a one-time fee for new members. This covers the cost of manuals and other materials associated with new members.
