About Us
Fort Collins Toastmasters is the oldest club in Colorado, founded in 1946. We are an open club and welcome all who want to improve their communication and leadership skills. We are a casual club but still follow the Toastmaster's meeting format.
Meeting location
Homestate Bank, 303 East Mountian Avenue, Fort Collins CO: We meet in the basement.
Meeting time
Arrive at 6:00 PM for setup; meeting starts promptly at 6:30 PM. Meeting ends at 8:00 PM.

Membership fees details
Like all TM clubs, we have dues:
  • $42 per six months, and is pro-rated as needed.
  • $6 of that goes to the club.
  • The remainder goes to Toastmasters International, and covers the monthly magazine, helps fund conferences and similar administrivia and interests in common.
  • Toastmasters International also imposes a $26 one-time fee for new members. This covers the cost of manuals and other materials associated with new members.
